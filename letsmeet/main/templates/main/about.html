What is letsmeet.click?

Letsmeet is a platform to organize your events and get informed about events you might be interested in.

You can:

  • Subscribe to communities to tell your interest in events
  • Comment on events to plan details
  • Tell others that you are coming/not coming to an event
  • Easily share your communities and events in social media
  • Enrich your communities and events with useful info like homepage adress, twitter username, etc.
  • Get notifications (email) about new events, comments, RSVPs and more

and more...

Coming soon:

  • Add locations to events
  • iCal export to import events into your calendar
  • RSS export to import events into your reader
  • Search to find stuff that might interest you
  • More ways to get notified about stuff
For more check our roadmap milestones
